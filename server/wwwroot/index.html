How to Play

Collect the fruit for points, but avoid the wasps! Wasp stings will disable the sloth for a short time.

Complete words to get additional time.

The game ends when the timer bar runs out.

Controls

Left and Right arrow keys move a sloth.

Spacebar drops a sloth from its branch.

Note 1: there is sound and music.

Note 2: needs a modern browser.
